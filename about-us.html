About Us

About Us

We are an experienced mining services organization engaged in the execution of integrated coal mining projects, including overburden removal, Mine Development and Operations (MDO), and end-to-end coal logistics. Acting as a principal contractor, we undertake complete responsibility for mine development, production, and coal evacuation in accordance with approved mine plans and statutory provisions.

Our operations are carried out with strict adherence to DGMS guidelines, environmental clearances, labour laws, and structured safety management systems. Supported by well-maintained Heavy Earth Moving Machinery (HEMM), trained manpower, and defined operational controls, we deliver assured production, consistent performance, and efficient resource utilization while contributing to national energy security through responsible mining practices.
